Meet the Team Inside the Robot

Four specialists who work together every time you ask a question
🧠
The Writer
Drafts the question
Reads your English question and writes it as a database instruction — but never touches the data itself.
🛡️
The Guard
Checks it's safe
Follows a fixed checklist, every time. If the instruction could change or delete anything, it gets stopped right here.
🚀
The Runner
Fetches the facts
Takes the approved instruction to the database and brings back the real numbers — nothing invented.
📣
The Narrator
Tells the story
Turns the raw numbers into a short, plain-English answer, and always says what to watch out for.
